Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract? ▼
Nope! All memberships are month-to-month. No contracts, no commitments, no cancellation fees. We earn your membership every month.
What are your hours? ▼
Monday–Thursday: 5 AM – 9 PM, Friday: 5 AM – 7 PM, Saturday: 7 AM – 5 PM, Sunday: 9 AM – 3 PM. Early birds and night owls welcome.
Can I try the gym before joining? ▼
Absolutely! We offer free tours and day passes. Walk in anytime or chat with us below to schedule. No pressure — we want you to feel at home before you commit.
Do you offer military/veteran discounts? ▼
Yes! We have special veteran memberships available. Just ask at the front desk or chat with us below for details. Thank you for your service.
Where are you located? ▼
4665 SW Freeway, Houston TX 77027. Easy access from the Southwest Freeway with plenty of free parking. You can also call us at (713) 528-4200.
